Get Your Akan Name So Fast!!!!!

In Ghana, children are given their first name as a day name so this web page is an application whereby the application takes the user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and depending on their gender, they get their Akan name.

  • Wednesday:Akua
  • Thursday:Yaa
  • Friday:Afua
  • Saturday:Ama
