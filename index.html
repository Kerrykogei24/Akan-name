Akan Names

In Ghana, children are given their first name as a day name so this web page is where the application takes the user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and depending on their gender they get their Akan name.

Here are some Ghananian names;

FEMALE:

  • Sunday:Akosua
  • Monday:Adwoa
  • Tuesday:Abenaa
  • Wednesday:Akua
  • Thursday:Yaa
  • Friday:Afua
  • Saturday:Ama

MALE:

  • Sunday:Kwasi
  • Monday:Kwadwo
  • Tuesday:Kwabena
  • Wednesday:Kwaku
  • Thursday:Yaw
  • Friday:Kofi
  • Saturday:Kwame
